How It Works

It's Painting time!

For those of you that, like us here at Paint Box, just can't decide what you want to paint next, we are your solution. Sharpen your hobby knives and prepare your palletes! Once a month you'll recieve a package containing a troop choice level box of miniatures from one of Games Workshop's thematic and detailed model ranges.

  • Choose from Warhammer: 40,000, Age of Sigmar, or both!
  • Choose which factions you want models from (minimum 4 per universe)
  • Elite and Single Entity Models every 4 months of subscription!
